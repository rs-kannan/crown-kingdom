OUR PURPOSE

why us?

The gold-making process involves several key steps:

Mining: Gold ore is extracted from the earth, usually through underground or open-pit mining. Crushing and Milling: The ore is crushed and ground to release gold particles. Concentration: Gold is separated from other minerals using gravity, flotation, or cyanidation. Smelting and Refining: The concentrated gold is melted in a furnace to remove impurities, resulting in pure gold. Refining can be done using chemical methods, such as electrolysis, to achieve even higher purity. Forming: The refined gold is shaped into bars, coins, or jewelry, depending on its intended use.
